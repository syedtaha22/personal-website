Hobbies and Interests

Explore creativity through photography, writing, 3D art, and teaching.

Photography

Photography is one of my passions. Capturing moments and telling stories through the lens is an art form I deeply enjoy. Whether it's landscapes, portraits, or candid shots, photography allows me to see the world from different perspectives.

I often spend my weekends exploring new places, always on the lookout for the perfect shot. Photography not only hones my observational skills but also provides a creative outlet to express my view of the world.

Writing

Writing is another significant part of my life. It enables me to articulate my thoughts, share experiences, and create imaginative worlds. From short stories to blog posts, writing is a way for me to communicate and connect with others.

Whether it's journaling my daily experiences or crafting fictional tales, writing helps me process my thoughts and emotions. It's a therapeutic practice that enhances my creativity and clarity of thought.

3D Art (Using Blender)

Creating 3D art using Blender is a fascinating journey into the realm of digital artistry. From modeling to texturing and rendering, every step of the process is a learning experience. Blender allows me to bring my ideas to life in three dimensions.

Working on 3D projects sharpens my technical skills and expands my artistic horizons. The possibilities are endless, and I enjoy experimenting with different styles and techniques to create unique digital art pieces.

Teaching

Teaching is not just a profession for me; it's a passion. Sharing knowledge and helping others grow brings immense satisfaction. Whether it's tutoring students, conducting workshops, or mentoring peers, teaching allows me to make a positive impact on others' lives.

I believe in continuous learning and strive to inspire the same in my students. Teaching keeps me engaged, curious, and constantly improving. It's a rewarding experience that fuels my desire to learn and grow.

Exploring Creativity

Whether it's through the lens of a camera, the stroke of a pen, the manipulation of digital pixels, or the act of teaching, creativity is at the heart of everything I do. It drives my passion and fuels my enthusiasm for life.

My journey into the world of 3D art using Blender has been particularly exciting. Blender allows me to transform my imagination into visual art, creating stunning and intricate digital scenes. The process of modeling, texturing, and rendering in Blender is both challenging and rewarding, pushing me to continuously refine my skills and explore new techniques.

One of my recent works features three doors that lead to vastly different places: one to Earth from space, another to a different planet, and the third to the depths of the ocean. This piece symbolizes the endless possibilities that creativity and curiosity can unlock. Each project in Blender not only enhances my technical abilities but also expands my artistic horizons, allowing me to express complex ideas and narratives through digital art.
